# What does Urbancrest believe about the Bible?

## Short Answer

The Bible is God's inspired, truthful, and authoritative Word.

## Detailed Answer

The Scriptures reveal God's character, His saving work, and His will for faith and life. Scripture is the final authority for doctrine and practice.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
