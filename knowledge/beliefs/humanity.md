# What does Urbancrest believe about humanity?

## Short Answer

Every person is made in God's image, but all people are affected by sin.

## Detailed Answer

Human beings have dignity because they are created by God. Through sin, humanity became separated from God and unable to save itself.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
