# What does Urbancrest believe about religious liberty?

## Short Answer

Every person should be free to seek God and practice faith without coercion from government or religious institutions.

## Detailed Answer

Church and state have distinct responsibilities. Government should protect freedom of conscience and should not control the church.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
