# Should I get baptized again?

## Short Answer

A person should consider baptism if a previous baptism happened before personal faith in Jesus or did not reflect believer's baptism.

## Detailed Answer

The key question is whether the earlier baptism followed a genuine profession of faith in Christ. A pastor can help you think through your situation biblically.
