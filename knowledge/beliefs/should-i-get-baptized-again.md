# Should I get baptized again?

## Short Answer

If you were baptized before personally trusting in Jesus, Urbancrest would encourage you to be baptized as a believer after your profession of faith.

## Detailed Answer

Many people were baptized or dedicated as infants in another tradition. Urbancrest respects the spiritual importance that may have had for a family. However, we understand New Testament baptism as an act of obedience that follows a personal profession of faith in Jesus Christ. If you were baptized before you personally trusted Christ, or if your baptism did not reflect believer's baptism, a pastor can help you think through your next step.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest Church wants to help people know Jesus, grow in faith, and live on mission together. When a question involves a personal next step, the best place to begin is usually a Connect Card, contacting a pastor or staff member, or using the appropriate Church Center form.
