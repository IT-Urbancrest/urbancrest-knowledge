# What does Urbancrest believe about peace and war?

## Short Answer

Christians should pursue peace and seek to resolve conflict according to the teachings of Jesus.

## Detailed Answer

Believers should pray for peace, love their enemies, and work for righteousness while recognizing the responsibilities of civil government.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
