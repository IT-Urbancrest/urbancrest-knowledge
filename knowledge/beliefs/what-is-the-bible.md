# What is the Bible?

## Short Answer

The Bible is God's inspired, trustworthy, and authoritative Word. It is the final authority for what we believe and how we live.

## Detailed Answer

Urbancrest believes that Scripture was written by human authors under the guidance of the Holy Spirit. Because it is God's Word, it is true and trustworthy. The Bible reveals who God is, what He has done, the reality of sin, the way of salvation through Jesus Christ, and how believers should live. Doctrinal statements are helpful summaries, but they are not equal to Scripture. The Bible alone is the final authority.
