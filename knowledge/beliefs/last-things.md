# What does Urbancrest believe about the future?

## Short Answer

Jesus Christ will return personally and visibly, the dead will be raised, and God will judge all people.

## Detailed Answer

Those who belong to Christ will live forever with God. Those who reject Him will face eternal judgment. God will make all things new.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
