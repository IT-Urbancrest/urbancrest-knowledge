# What is repentance?

## Short Answer

Repentance is a genuine turning from sin and turning to God. It is more than feeling sorry. It is a change of heart and direction that accompanies saving faith in Jesus Christ.

## Detailed Answer

Biblical repentance includes recognizing sin, grieving over sin, turning away from sin, and turning toward God. Repentance does not mean a person becomes perfect before coming to Jesus. It means they stop defending sin and trust Christ as Lord. True repentance is produced by God's grace and leads to a changed life over time. Repentance and faith belong together. A person turns from sin and trusts Christ.
