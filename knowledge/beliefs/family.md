# What does Urbancrest believe about the family?

## Short Answer

God established the family as a foundational institution of human society.

## Detailed Answer

Marriage is the covenant union of one man and one woman. Husbands and wives are equal in worth and called to faithful, sacrificial love. Parents should teach their children to know and follow the Lord.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
