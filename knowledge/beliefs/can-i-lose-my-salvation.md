# Can I lose my salvation?

## Short Answer

Everyone who has truly been saved by Christ is kept by God's power and will persevere in faith.

## Detailed Answer

A genuine believer may struggle or wander, but God disciplines, restores, and preserves His children. Eternal security is not permission to live in unrepentant sin.
