# What does Urbancrest believe about evangelism and missions?

## Short Answer

Every follower of Jesus and every church is called to make disciples of all nations.

## Detailed Answer

Christians share the gospel through words and lives shaped by Christ, relying on the Holy Spirit and seeking the salvation of people everywhere.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
