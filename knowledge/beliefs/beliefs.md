# What We Believe

Urbancrest Church believes the Bible is God's inspired and authoritative Word and that it reveals His plan of salvation through Jesus Christ. The following summaries reflect the doctrines affirmed in the Baptist Faith and Message 2000 and serve as an overview of our biblical beliefs.

---

# 1. The Scriptures

**What do you believe about the Bible?**

We believe the Bible is God's inspired, infallible, and trustworthy Word. Human authors wrote under the guidance of the Holy Spirit, making Scripture completely true and the final authority for faith, doctrine, and daily living.

---

# 2. God

**Who is God?**

There is one true and living God who exists eternally as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. These three Persons are equal in nature, power, and glory while remaining one God.

### God the Father

God the Father reigns over all creation with wisdom, holiness, justice, mercy, and love. He desires a relationship with those who trust in Christ.

### God the Son

Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man. He was born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, rose bodily from the dead, ascended into heaven, and will return again.

### God the Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit convicts people of sin, draws them to Christ, indwells every believer, empowers Christian living, gives spiritual gifts, and produces spiritual fruit.

---

# 3. Humanity

**What do you believe about people?**

Every person is created in the image of God and possesses inherent dignity and value. Because of Adam's sin, every person inherits a sinful nature and is separated from God apart from salvation in Jesus Christ.

---

# 4. Salvation

**How is someone saved?**

Salvation is entirely by God's grace through faith in Jesus Christ.

Salvation includes:

* Regeneration (new birth)
* Justification (declared righteous)
* Sanctification (growing in Christ)
* Glorification (future perfection with Christ)

Salvation cannot be earned through good works, religious rituals, or personal effort.

---

# 5. God's Purpose of Grace

**Can a believer lose their salvation?**

Those who have genuinely trusted Christ are kept by God's power and will persevere in faith until the end. True believers are eternally secure because salvation depends upon God's grace rather than human effort.

---

# 6. The Church

**What is the Church?**

The Church is the body of Christ composed of all believers.

Each local church is an autonomous congregation of baptized believers who gather to:

* Worship God
* Study Scripture
* Observe baptism and the Lord's Supper
* Make disciples
* Share the Gospel
* Serve others

---

# 7. Baptism and the Lord's Supper

**What is baptism?**

Baptism is an act of obedience for believers after salvation. Urbancrest Church practices believer's baptism by immersion as a public declaration of faith in Jesus Christ.

**What is the Lord's Supper?**

The Lord's Supper is a symbolic remembrance of Christ's sacrifice and a proclamation of His death until He returns.

---

# 8. The Lord's Day

**Why do Christians gather on Sunday?**

The first day of the week commemorates Christ's resurrection. Christians gather regularly for worship, prayer, fellowship, Bible teaching, generosity, and service.

---

# 9. The Kingdom

**What is the Kingdom of God?**

God's Kingdom includes His sovereign rule over all creation and the spiritual reign of Christ in the lives of believers. The Kingdom will be fully realized when Christ returns.

---

# 10. Last Things

**What happens after death?**

Christ will personally return to earth.

There will be:

* The resurrection of all people
* Final judgment
* Eternal life for believers
* Eternal separation from God for those who reject Christ

God will create a new heaven and a new earth where believers will dwell with Him forever.

---

# 11. Evangelism and Missions

**Why does the Church share the Gospel?**

Jesus commanded His followers to make disciples of all nations. Every believer is called to share the Gospel through both words and actions until Christ returns.

---

# 12. Education

**Why is biblical teaching important?**

Followers of Christ should continually grow in biblical knowledge, wisdom, and spiritual maturity. Christian education equips believers to love God and serve others faithfully.

---

# 13. Stewardship

**What is stewardship?**

Everything we possess belongs to God.

Believers are called to faithfully steward:

* Time
* Talents
* Spiritual gifts
* Finances
* Opportunities

Generosity is an act of worship and obedience.

---

# 14. Cooperation

**Why do churches cooperate together?**

Local churches voluntarily partner with other like-minded churches to advance the Gospel more effectively through missions, church planting, education, disaster relief, and ministry.

---

# 15. The Christian and the Social Order

**How should Christians impact society?**

Christians are called to love their neighbors, pursue justice, defend human dignity, care for those in need, and influence society according to biblical truth while proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

---

# 16. Peace and War

**What does the Bible teach about peace?**

Christians should seek peace whenever possible while recognizing the responsibility of governments to uphold justice and protect human life.

---

# 17. Religious Liberty

**Do you believe in religious freedom?**

Every person should have the freedom to worship according to conscience without coercion. Church and state have distinct roles under God's authority.

---

# 18. The Family

**What does the Bible teach about the family?**

The family is God's foundational institution for society.

Marriage is the covenant union of one man and one woman.

Parents are responsible for raising their children in the instruction and discipline of the Lord, while children are called to honor and obey their parents.

Every family member possesses equal worth before God.

---

# Frequently Asked Questions

## What does Urbancrest Church believe?

Urbancrest Church affirms the doctrines summarized in the Baptist Faith and Message 2000 and believes the Bible is God's inspired, authoritative, and sufficient Word.

---

## Are you a Baptist church?

Yes. Urbancrest Church is affiliated with the Southern Baptist Convention and holds to the Baptist Faith and Message 2000 as a summary of biblical doctrine.

---

## Is the Baptist Faith and Message equal to the Bible?

No. The Bible alone is our final authority. The Baptist Faith and Message serves as a summary of what we believe the Bible teaches.

---

## Why do you baptize by immersion?

Because we believe the New Testament teaches believer's baptism by immersion following a personal profession of faith in Jesus Christ.

---

## Do you believe salvation is by grace alone?

Yes. Salvation is entirely by God's grace through faith in Jesus Christ apart from works.

---

## Do you believe Jesus is God?

Yes. Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man, the eternal Son of God and the only Savior of the world.

---

## Do you believe the Bible is true?

Yes. We believe the Bible is inspired by God, completely trustworthy, and the final authority for faith and practice.

---

## Do you believe Jesus will return?

Yes. We believe Jesus Christ will personally and visibly return, judge the living and the dead, and establish His eternal kingdom.
