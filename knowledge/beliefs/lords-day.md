# What does Urbancrest believe about the Lord's Day?

## Short Answer

Christians gather on Sunday to worship because Jesus rose from the dead on the first day of the week.

## Detailed Answer

The Lord's Day is devoted to worship, fellowship, service, and activities consistent with Christian faith and conscience.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
