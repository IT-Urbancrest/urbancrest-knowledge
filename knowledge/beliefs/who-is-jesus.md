# Who is Jesus?

## Short Answer

Jesus is the eternal Son of God, fully God and fully man.

## Detailed Answer

He was born of a virgin, lived without sin, revealed the Father, died as the substitute for sinners, rose bodily from the grave, ascended to heaven, and will return.
