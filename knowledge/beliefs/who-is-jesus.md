# Who is Jesus?

## Short Answer

Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God, fully God and fully man. He lived without sin, died on the cross for sinners, rose bodily from the dead, and is the only Savior.

## Detailed Answer

The Bible teaches that Jesus is not merely a teacher, prophet, or moral example. He is the Son of God who came to reveal the Father and redeem sinners. He was born of a virgin, lived in perfect obedience, performed signs that revealed His authority, willingly died on the cross, and rose again. Jesus now reigns as Lord and will return. Because Jesus is fully God, His work is powerful to save. Because He became fully man, He is the perfect mediator for people who need forgiveness.
