# What does baptism mean?

## Short Answer

Baptism is a public act of obedience for believers that symbolizes union with Christ in His death, burial, and resurrection.

## Detailed Answer

Baptism does not save a person. Salvation comes by grace through faith in Jesus Christ. Baptism follows salvation as a visible testimony that someone belongs to Christ. Immersion in water pictures burial with Christ, and coming out of the water pictures being raised to walk in new life. Urbancrest practices believer's baptism by immersion because this best reflects the New Testament pattern and the meaning of the ordinance.
