# What does Urbancrest believe about the church?

## Short Answer

A local church is a congregation of baptized believers joined together under the lordship of Christ.

## Detailed Answer

The church gathers for worship, teaching, fellowship, prayer, ordinances, service, and mission. Jesus is the head of the church.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
