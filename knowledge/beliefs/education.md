# What does Urbancrest believe about Christian education?

## Short Answer

Christian education helps people understand Scripture and grow in faithful discipleship.

## Detailed Answer

Churches, families, and Christian institutions should teach truth with intellectual honesty, spiritual maturity, and submission to Christ.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
