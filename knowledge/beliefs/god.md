# What does Urbancrest believe about God?

## Short Answer

There is one living and true God who eternally exists as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

## Detailed Answer

God is holy, loving, all-knowing, all-powerful, and sovereign. The three Persons of the Trinity are fully God, yet God is one.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
