# What does Urbancrest believe about the Kingdom of God?

## Short Answer

God's Kingdom includes His rule over all creation and His saving rule in the lives of believers.

## Detailed Answer

Christians pray and work for God's will to be done while awaiting the visible return and final reign of Jesus.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
