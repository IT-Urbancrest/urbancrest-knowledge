# What does Urbancrest believe about Christians and society?

## Short Answer

Christians should seek justice, love their neighbors, defend human dignity, and live as faithful witnesses in society.

## Detailed Answer

Believers should oppose racism, greed, violence, exploitation, sexual immorality, and every form of injustice while showing grace and speaking truth.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
