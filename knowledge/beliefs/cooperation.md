# What does Urbancrest believe about cooperation?

## Short Answer

Churches may cooperate with other believers and organizations when doing so supports biblical ministry and preserves the church's convictions.

## Detailed Answer

Cooperation can strengthen missions, education, relief, and other gospel efforts. Each local church remains accountable to Christ.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
