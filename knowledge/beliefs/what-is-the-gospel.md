# What is the Gospel?

## Short Answer

The Gospel is the good news that Jesus Christ died for our sins, rose from the dead, and offers forgiveness and eternal life to all who repent and believe in Him.

## Detailed Answer

The word Gospel means good news. The good news begins with God, who created all things and made people in His image. Humanity sinned against God and cannot repair that separation through personal effort. Jesus came as fully God and fully man, lived a sinless life, died as the substitute for sinners, and rose again in victory over sin and death. The proper response to the Gospel is repentance and faith. The Gospel is not advice about how to become a better person. It is the announcement of what Christ has done and the invitation to trust Him as Lord and Savior.
