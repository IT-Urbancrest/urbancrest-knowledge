# What is the gospel?

## Short Answer

The gospel is the good news that Jesus Christ died for our sins and rose from the dead.

## Detailed Answer

God created us, but sin separates us from Him. Jesus, the Son of God, lived without sin, died in the place of sinners, and rose victorious. Everyone who repents and believes in Him receives forgiveness and eternal life.
