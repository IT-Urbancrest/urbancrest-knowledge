# What does Urbancrest believe about stewardship?

## Short Answer

Everything belongs to God, and Christians should faithfully use their time, abilities, possessions, and finances for His glory.

## Detailed Answer

Giving should be willing, generous, and joyful. Stewardship supports the church's ministry, care for others, and the spread of the gospel.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest's doctrinal framework is consistent with the Baptist Faith and Message 2000. Scripture remains the final authority.
