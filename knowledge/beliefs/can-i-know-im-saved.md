# Can I know I am saved?

## Short Answer

Yes. A believer can have assurance of salvation because salvation rests on the finished work of Jesus Christ, not on personal performance.

## Detailed Answer

Assurance is not based on being perfect or never struggling. It is based on God's promise to save those who repent and believe in Jesus. The Bible also teaches that genuine faith produces spiritual fruit over time, such as a desire to follow Christ, conviction of sin, love for other believers, and growth in obedience. Believers may experience seasons of doubt, but they can return to the promises of Scripture and the character of God. If someone is unsure, it is wise to talk with a pastor who can help them think biblically and pastorally.
