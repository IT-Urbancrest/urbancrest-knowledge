# How do I talk with a pastor?

## Short Answer

You can talk with a pastor by completing a Connect Card, calling the church office, or emailing staff directly.

## Detailed Answer

Whether you have questions about salvation, baptism, membership, a difficult life situation, or a ministry need, Urbancrest wants to help you connect with the right person. For urgent or sensitive matters, direct contact with the church office is best.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest Church wants to help people know Jesus, grow in faith, and live on mission together. When a question involves a personal next step, the best place to begin is usually a Connect Card, contacting a pastor or staff member, or using the appropriate Church Center form.

## Next Step

Complete a Connect Card or call the church office and ask to speak with a pastor.
