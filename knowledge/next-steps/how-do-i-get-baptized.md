# How do I get baptized?

## Short Answer

If you have trusted Jesus, complete the Baptism Interest Form to begin the process.

## Detailed Answer

A pastor or ministry leader will follow up to hear your testimony, answer questions, and help schedule baptism. Urbancrest practices believer's baptism by immersion.
