# How do I submit a prayer request?

## Short Answer

You can submit a prayer request by contacting the church, completing the appropriate form when available, or speaking with a pastor or staff member.

## Detailed Answer

Urbancrest believes prayer is an important part of church life. If your request is urgent, sensitive, or pastoral in nature, contacting the church office directly is best. The AI should not attempt to provide counseling beyond general encouragement and should direct personal needs to a pastor or ministry leader.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest Church wants to help people know Jesus, grow in faith, and live on mission together. When a question involves a personal next step, the best place to begin is usually a Connect Card, contacting a pastor or staff member, or using the appropriate Church Center form.

## Next Step

Use the prayer request link in Church Center when available or contact the church office.
