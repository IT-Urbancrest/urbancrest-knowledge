# How do I start serving?

## Short Answer

The best way to start serving is to fill out a Connect Card and let us know where you are interested in getting involved.

## Detailed Answer

Urbancrest has many serving opportunities across ministries, events, worship, production, kids, students, hospitality, missions, and more. Some roles may require training, background checks, or additional ministry approval, especially when working with children or students.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest Church wants to help people know Jesus, grow in faith, and live on mission together. When a question involves a personal next step, the best place to begin is usually a Connect Card, contacting a pastor or staff member, or using the appropriate Church Center form.

## Next Step

Complete a Connect Card, email staff directly, or call the church office.
