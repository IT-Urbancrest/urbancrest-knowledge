# How do I fill out a Connect Card?

## Short Answer

The Connect Card is the primary first step for getting connected at Urbancrest.

## Detailed Answer

A Connect Card helps our team learn more about you and follow up with the right information. You can use it if you are new, have a question, want to get involved, need prayer, or want help finding your next step.

## Urbancrest Information

At Urbancrest, the Connect Card is also used for general follow-up and prayer requests.
