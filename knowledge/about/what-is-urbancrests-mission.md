# What is Urbancrest's mission?

## Short Answer

Urbancrest's mission is Every nation. Every street. One mission.

## Detailed Answer

This mission expresses our commitment to make disciples locally and globally by helping people know Jesus, grow in faith, and live on mission together.

## Urbancrest Information

The mission guides worship, discipleship, ministry, outreach, and missions.
