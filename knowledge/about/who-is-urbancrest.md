# Who is Urbancrest Church?

## Short Answer

Urbancrest Church is a local church in Lebanon, Ohio, committed to helping people know Jesus, grow in faith, and live on mission together.

## Detailed Answer

Urbancrest is a Christian church centered on the gospel of Jesus Christ. We gather for worship, biblical teaching, prayer, fellowship, service, and mission.

## Urbancrest Information

Our mission is: Every nation. Every street. One mission. Helping people know Jesus, grow in faith, and live on mission together.
