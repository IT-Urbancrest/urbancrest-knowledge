# What should I wear to church?

## Short Answer

Wear what is comfortable and appropriate for a church gathering.

## Detailed Answer

People attend in a range of clothing styles. You do not need special clothes to visit.

## Urbancrest Information

Urbancrest values people more than a particular dress style.

## Next Step

Come as you are and focus on participating in worship.
