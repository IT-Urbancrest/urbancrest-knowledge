# What time are your services?

## Short Answer

Urbancrest meets every Sunday at **9:30 AM** and **11:00 AM**.

## Sunday Worship

Two identical worship services are offered each Sunday:

- 9:30 AM
- 11:00 AM

## Wednesday Evenings

- 5:30 PM – Free Dinner (Gymnasium)
- 6:15 PM – AWANA for Kids
- 6:30 PM – Student Ministry
- 6:30 PM – Adult Bible Studies
