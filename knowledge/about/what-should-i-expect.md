# What should I expect when I visit?

## Short Answer

You can expect a welcoming church gathering centered on worship, prayer, biblical teaching, and the gospel.

## Detailed Answer

A typical visit includes congregational singing, Scripture, prayer, a message from the Bible, and information about next steps. Volunteers can help you find where to go.

## Urbancrest Information

First-time guests can use guest parking and enter through the main entrance.

## Next Step

Complete the Connect Card after your visit or whenever you have questions.
