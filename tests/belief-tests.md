# Belief Retrieval Tests

- What is the gospel?
- How can I be saved?
- Can I lose my salvation?
- What does Urbancrest believe about the Bible?
- What does Urbancrest believe about marriage and family?
- What happens after death?
- Why should Christians care about missions?
- Is baptism required for salvation?

Expected behavior: Give a direct biblical answer, distinguish Scripture from confessional summaries, and recommend an appropriate next step only when useful.
