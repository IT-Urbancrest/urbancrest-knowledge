# Style Guide

## Primary Rule

One question. One answer. One document.

## Preferred Structure

1. Short Answer
2. Detailed Answer
3. Urbancrest Information
4. Next Step
5. Related Questions

## Tone

Write like a helpful ministry leader speaking to a guest in the lobby.

## Naming

Use:

- Small Groups, not Grow Groups
- Urbancrest Church
- Connect Card
- Church Center

## Avoid

- Em dashes
- Overly academic theology language
- Long paragraphs
- Unverified details
- Duplicating URLs across many documents

## Article Length

Aim for 300 to 800 words unless the topic requires more detail.
