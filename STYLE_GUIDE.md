# Style Guide

## Writing

- Use short paragraphs.
- Prefer plain language.
- Avoid church jargon unless it is explained.
- Do not use em dashes.
- Do not use the word meaningful as a filler.
- Use Small Groups, never Grow Groups.
- Refer to Urbancrest Church as Urbancrest after the first use.
- Use believer's baptism by immersion when describing baptism.

## Theology

- Scripture is the final authority.
- The Baptist Faith and Message 2000 is a helpful doctrinal summary, not equal to Scripture.
- Salvation is by grace alone through faith alone in Jesus Christ alone.
- Baptism follows salvation and does not save.
